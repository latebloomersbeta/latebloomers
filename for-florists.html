Why it works for florists
💷

Extra revenue, zero effort

Flowers you would have binned at the end of the day now earn you money. List in under a minute, collect payment automatically.

🌿

Less waste, better conscience

Every bunch you rescue is one less trip to the bin. Good for the planet, good for your brand, good for the soul.

🗺️

New local customers find you

Late Bloomers brings people to your door who never knew you existed. Many come back as full-price regulars.

✅

Free to join

No upfront cost, no subscription. We take a small cut only when you make a sale. If you do not sell, you pay nothing.

🏪

You stay in control

You set your own prices and availability. List when you want, hide when you do not. No pressure, no commitments.

🌸

A curated community

How it works

Simple as that.

No complicated setup. You are listing and selling within minutes of joining.

1

Get in touch

Drop Clem a message on WhatsApp or email. She will get back to you the same day.

2

Get listed

We set up your shop profile together. Takes about 10 minutes. Your shop goes live on the platform.

3

List your stems

At the end of the day, pop into your dashboard and list whatever you have left. Set a price and a collect-by time.

4

Get paid

Customers reserve and pay in the app. You hand over the flowers, we transfer your earnings every week.
